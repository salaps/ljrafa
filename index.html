HOW TO USE AN ELFBAR? STEP BY STEP.

1. INHALE STEADILY

With any vape device, the trick for an effective draw is by inhaling steadily. Don't breathe in too quickly, especially during those first couple of puffs. With a new vape, take a few short puffs and then a long drag of 3-5 seconds. This will help improve the flavor of the draw.

2. HOLD THE VAPOR

Vaping is much like smoking a cigar if you've tried them before. Holding the vapor in your mouth means that the vapor doesn't need to go directly into the lungs to create the desired effect. Hold the vapor in your mouth for a few seconds.

3. WAIT UNTIL YOU
FEEL THE HIT

Before you exhale, wait for the hit. It's important to then wait 15-30 seconds between that draw and the next one to help saturate the wick inside the device.Otherwise, it can run the risk of a dry hit, which let us tell you - doesn't taste great.
